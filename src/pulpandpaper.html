PULP & PAPER
  24/7/365

Plant shutdowns that are scheduled require careful planning to execute the movement of parts without delays at customs, brokers, the border, airlines and warehouses especially after hours, weekends and holidays.

Freight forwarders are challenged to manage the process outside of regular business hours and to “think outside of the box”.

Unscheduled shutdowns require a next flight service and mentality as every hour delayed incurs significant losses. Our ability to respond 24/7/365 ensures downtime is minimized.

Emergency Parts Logistics team can help you design a program that will give your company the ability to overcome many of your current supply management issues during shutdowns.

This custom tailored program will avoid unnecessary delays with customs, brokers, airlines and warehouses especially after hours, holidays and weekends.

Our premium door to door services provide the Pulp & Paper Industry solutions to their most challenging domestic and international shipping requests during unscheduled and scheduled plant shutdowns. Whether you are importing emergency JIT inventory or importing/exporting an emergency part for a “line down” situation, Emergency Parts Logistics offers the fastest transit times.

Our Cargo Services Group keeps your costs down by using routings and services that allow for more time to have freight delivered. At the same time we have the ability to expedite the shipment without further delays.
