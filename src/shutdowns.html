Plant Shutdowns are Complex
  24/7/365

Emergency Parts Logistics Inc focuses on one challenge only: ensuring your deadlines are met. When you have a shutdown to manage, we have all the processes in place to ensure that there are no delays with customs, brokers, airlines and warehouses.

Most freight companies have no problems managing the movement of goods during regular business hours. The real challenge is to move freight after hours, avoid customs delays, knowing who to contact on a national holiday and ensuring access to your freight 24 hours a day.

Our expertise, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week minimizes delays that allow our customers to execute shutdowns on schedule and avoid penalties. As your parts move general cargo, managed by our Cargo Services Group, we keep costs down.

Mission Critical

EPL will ensure your freight can be elevated to a “next flight out” status immediately without waiting for the next business day to start making arrangements.

Our goal is to manage your shutdowns with no unanticipated delays, be cost effective, flexible and provide streamlined processes that will add value and ultimately facilitate in accelerating the process as required.

Our corporate culture is “Mission Critical” at all times even when your freight does not require a quick turn around.
