NEXT FLIGHT OUT
  24/7/365

Organizations in the Aerospace, Mining, Oil & Gas, Pulp & Paper, and High-tech industries will eventually require a next flight out service. Any courier can put your time critical shipment on a flight and send it on its way. The real challenge is to ensure there are no delays, no penalties and no loss of revenues, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Over the years, our team at Emergency Parts Logistics Inc has moved 1000's of time critical shipments in different industries, providing us with real life experiences globally.

At Emergency Parts Logistics Inc, we create detailed SOP’s for all parties prior to any next flight out situation. Our approach will avoid lost time due to communication issues and improper routings.

We ensure that your shipment is moving at all times with no delays. The routings we determine to be the most efficient have the right equipment, freight is accessible, transships are achievable and deadlines are met.
