► MINING

There is a real challenge expediting time critical shipments to remote mines. Un-scheduled shutdowns incurring loss of revenues requires careful consideration of a carrier that can minimize downtime instead of making the situation worse.

Emergency Parts Logistics specializes in moving parts the fastest way possible. We are experts in planning and coordinating routings, avoiding customs delays and communicating to all parties at all times. We have developed the most sophisticated proprietary track and trace program that allows our customers to see the planned routing and ETA from either a desk top computer or a smart phone.

We are available 24/7/365 to respond to your most challenging and difficult shipping requests. We offer four services;

► Direct drives
► Next flight out
► Hand carry
► Charters
